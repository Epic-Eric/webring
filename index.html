The Unnamed Webring

A webring for University of Toronto Engineering students and the broader UofT community.

This webring is an attempt to inspire students, faculty, and alumni of UofT to build their own websites and share traffic amongst each other. The ring welcomes personal websites, portfolios, and blogs.

What is a webring?

A webring is a collection of websites linked together in a circular structure. In the early days of the web, webrings were a popular way to discover related websites. Each site in the ring includes a navigation widget that lets visitors travel to the next or previous site in the ring.

The Unnamed Webring revives this tradition for the UofT community, creating a network of personal websites and projects by students, faculty, and alumni.

The current UofT community and Alumni network is thousands-strong -- why not harness this immense network?

Joining the Webring

Add the webring widget to your website HTML (template below). Generally, you should add it to the footer.

Fork this repo and add your information to the bottom of the members array in js/webring-data.js following this format:

{
  "name": "Your Name",
  "website": "https://your-website.com",
  "faculty": "Your Faculty (e.g., Engineering, Arts & Science, etc.)",
  "designation": "Your Role (e.g., Undergrad, Grad, Faculty, etc.)",
  "year": "1-25",  // Month and year added to the webring (e.g., 1-25 for January 2025)
  "grad": "2T5"    // Expected graduation year in UofT format (e.g., 2T5, 2T8)
}

Submit a Pull Request! We'll try to review as fast as we can.

    <a href="https://webring.ayanali.net/#your-site-here" target="_blank">
        <img src="https://webring.ayanali.net/img/icon.svg" alt="The Unnamed Webring" style="width: 32px; height: 32px;"/>
    </a>
    <a href="https://webring.ayanali.net/#your-site-here?nav=next" style="color: #333; text-decoration: none; font-size: 1.5rem;">→</a>
</div>

Questions?

Who can join?
Any UofT student, alumni, faculty, or staff with a personal website.
My website isn't tech-related, can I still join?
Absolutely! The webring welcomes websites about any topic.
How do I report issues?
Please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
